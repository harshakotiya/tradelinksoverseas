About Us

We are an export house striving to provide finest quality food grains to our customers.

Welcome To Tradelinks Overseas

Here at Tradelinks Overseas, we believe in the idea of building a relationship with our customers over the foundations of trust and faith. And we make it possible through our transparent procedures that we undertake to get you the quality of products that you deserve- the best, of course. To send across the finest quality of nutrient-rich food products to the customers, we make sure that the entire process right from acquiring the products to delivering the packaged items is genuinely safe and reliable.

Living in a world where quality degradation doubles as a money-making machine, it's hard to rely on just any seller claiming food products to be pure and safe. But at Tradelinks Overseas, we believe in fulfilling the highest standards for quality, consistency, dependability, value-added support services and integrity in our business and personal relationships.

The company is having good associations with some worldwide companies and we have developed a large network of clientele which is spread all across the globe. Some of the major countries where we export are Sri Lanka , UAE, China and Australia. Our customers are fully satisfied by our products and services which represents our high quality standards and reliability. The company strives to provide products of the highest quality that give maximum value for money.
